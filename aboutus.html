About Us

HISTORY

First established as a small shop in Jakarta in 2018, currently it has three branches, namely in Jakarta, Bandung, and Sanur. CHOCS provides many variants of chocolate products that chocolate lovers will definitely like.

As Indonesian chocolatiers with a rich history, we started selling homemade chocolates in Kebon Jeruk, West Jakarta in 2018. After some years of crafting fine chocolates, you get to know a thing or two about doing it right.

INGREDIENTS

At CHOCS, We source premium-quality ingredients from around the world, and choose local options whenever possible. We never compromise on quality, and you bet you can taste it.

Our chocolates are made with high-quality cocoa Belgian Chocolate, aromatic Mediterranean Hazelnut, and the thick and rich Milk. We pay a premium for sustainable cocoa to ensure our farmer partners and co-ops are supported by programs that improve their profit and the livelihoods of their families. It's really quite simple, it's a choice we've made because it's the right choice to make.
